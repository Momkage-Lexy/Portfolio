Frequently Asked Questions

What are your ticket prices?

General admission starts at $89.99 for adults and $69.99 for children. Season passes and group discounts are available. Check our website for current promotions and special offers!

Do you offer accessibility services?

Absolutely! ThrillVerse is committed to providing an inclusive experience. We offer wheelchair rentals, companion assistance programs, and accessible entrances to most attractions. Contact us for specific accessibility needs.

Can I bring food and drinks into the park?

Small snacks and non-alcoholic beverages in non-glass containers are permitted. We also have amazing dining options throughout the park featuring everything from quick bites to full-service restaurants.

What should I do if I lose something in the park?

Visit our Guest Services center near the main entrance or call our Lost & Found hotline at (555) 123-LOST. We keep found items for 90 days and can ship items to you if needed.

Do you have group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 15 or more receive special pricing and can enjoy reserved seating areas, dedicated entrances, and meal packages. Contact our Group Sales team for customized packages.
