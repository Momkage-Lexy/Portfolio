About Me

I'm Lexy, a dreamer and coder with a passion for crafting digital experiences that make sense. With a background in computer science, I blend traditional aesthetics with modern technology.


My journey began in a small coastal town, where I spent my childhood collecting seashells and sketching in weathered notebooks. These early experiences shaped my artistic vision, infusing my work with a sense of timeless beauty and whispered memories.


When I'm not staring at a screen, you can find me tending to my tbr list, experimenting with crochet patterns, or wandering through forgotten places in search of inspiration.
