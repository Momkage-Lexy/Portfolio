Featured Projects

A closer look at some of my favorite creative works, where dreams meet design.

Whispering Pines

Whispering Pines is a boutique hotel nestled in the mountains, where guests escape to reconnect with nature. The branding needed to capture both the rugged beauty of the landscape and the refined comfort of the accommodations.

Challenge

The hotel wanted to distinguish itself from typical rustic mountain lodges while still honoring its natural surroundings. We needed to create a brand that felt both elevated and approachable, luxurious yet authentic.

Solution

I developed a visual identity centered around custom illustrations of local flora and fauna, paired with a refined color palette inspired by the changing mountain light. The logo combines elegant typography with subtle organic elements.

The complete branding package included logo design, stationery, signage, website design, and marketing materials. Each piece maintains continuity while allowing for creative variations that keep the brand feeling fresh and dynamic.

Moonflower Journal

Moonflower Journal is a literary magazine celebrating poetic prose and delicate illustrations. The publication needed a design that would honor the written word while creating space for visual storytelling.

Challenge

The editors wanted each issue to feel like a cherished book while maintaining magazine functionality. The design needed to accommodate diverse content—from long-form essays to short poems—without feeling disjointed.

Solution

I created a flexible layout system with distinct treatments for different content types, unified by a consistent typographic hierarchy and delicate decorative elements. Each section opens with a custom illustrated initial cap.

The design has helped Moonflower Journal develop a devoted readership, with many subscribers keeping complete collections of the beautifully designed issues. The publication has been recognized in several design competitions for its elegant approach to literary magazine design.

Velvet & Lace

Velvet & Lace is a vintage-inspired fashion boutique specializing in romantic, feminine designs. Their e-commerce presence needed to reflect the tactile luxury of their garments while providing a seamless shopping experience.

Challenge

The website needed to convey the luxurious feel of the boutique's physical location while maintaining fast load times and mobile responsiveness. Product presentation was crucial to compensate for customers not being able to touch the fabrics.

Solution

I designed a rich visual experience with subtle animations that mimic fabric movement. High-quality imagery is complemented by detailed texture close-ups. The color scheme and typography evoke vintage elegance while maintaining modern readability.

Since launch, the website has contributed to a 40% increase in online sales and has become a key part of the brand's identity. The design successfully bridges the gap between digital convenience and the tactile luxury of vintage fashion.
