Creative Projects

A closer look at some of my favorite creative works, where dreams meet design.

Quimby's Books

Quimby's is a "Victorian flavored" bookshop soon to open in Independence, Oregon. The shop is located in the historic district of town and will offer all types of books with a specialty in victorian era content. Since this business is in development I decided to draw up some mock design plans for their website

Ovenbird Bakery

Ovenbird Bakery is a long standing local business in my town. Their online presence ebbs and flows, and currently no website is active. These are mock plans for a website for them and a potential creative plan for other online marketing use.

FairyFloss

FairyFloss is an imaginary cotton candy food truck with locations all over Oregon. They wanted to refresh their webpage utilizing the bright, colorful (&sweet) nature of their work.
